    \section{Material properties}
    \subsection{Stress}
    The stress is defined as force applied divided by cross-sectional area and denoted as $\sigma$
    \begin{equation}
        \sigma = \frac{F}{A}
    \end{equation}
    Hence, stress is measured in $Nm^{-2}$, which is called $Pa$ (scals).
